专业介绍 Major introduction

本人本科专业为智能医学工程，专注于研究人工智能如何赋能医学领域，通过AI技术解决传统医疗无法解决的问题，提升医疗服务质量与效率。
主修课程有：深度学习、计算机操作系统、统计学、数据分析与可视化、数据结构、机器学习、数据库原理、医学图像处理、临床医学概论等。

My undergraduate major is Intelligent Medical Engineering. I focus on researching how artificial intelligence empowers the medical field, addressing challenges that traditional healthcare cannot tackle through AI technologies, and improving the quality and efficiency of medical services.
Core Courses Included: Deep Learning, Computer Operating Systems, Statistics, Data Analysis and Visualization, Data Structures, Machine Learning, Database Principles, Medical Image Processing, Introduction to Clinical Medicine, etc.
